I am really looking forward to thanksgiving this year for two reasons. First, it will allow me to dedicate some additonal time time to my coding bootcamp. Maintianing a full time job while learning to code has been challenging. Second, I am in desperate need of a new phone and it seems like waiting for a black friday deal seems like my best bet. Unfortuantly, the consensus on the pixel 4 is that its a miss by google. Small battery, half baked features, and all around a dissapointment. Plus the lack of a headphone jack is still a major paint point for me. Why a flagship phone running stock (or near stock) android with a large battery and a headphone jack cant be found in 2019 is beyond me.
